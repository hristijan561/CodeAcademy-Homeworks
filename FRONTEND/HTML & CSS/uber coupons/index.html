The first stop to saving

Download your Rider app: Apple with iOS 8 or newer - App Store Android 4.0.3 - Google Play Store Windows at Microsoft.com Now you are set and ready in every way, for money off with Uber codes to cash back with credit cards.

Uber Visa Credit Card - value!

Their Visa Credit Card is a king among cards: no annual fee and up to 4% rewards. Do you eat out often, travel for work, shop online and pay your credit card balance in full every month? Then this card is a must. Let it save in the background while Uber coupons do the work upfront. Get $100 back when you spend $500. You have a whole 90 days to reach that total. Tip: Get the card to cover a period you expect to be paying this for your Uber use. Then save high percentage points on every net $1 you spend: 4% back/4 points at restaurants, bars, and UberEATS 3% back/3 points on airfares, hotels and home rentals like Airbnb 2% back/2 points shopping online, such as for video and streaming 1% back/1 point on all spending outside of these bonus categories The rewards don't end there, with extra freebies coming your way: Earn up to $50 subscription credit for digital streaming purchases. Spend $5000+ in your first 12 months Coverage for up to $600 for mobile phone damage or theft for each phone on the wireless account. Pay your mobile phone bill with your card No foreign transaction fees when using the card outside the U.S. How to apply? Apply through the app for approval within a few minutes. That's it. Your physical card will arrive within 1 to 2 weeks of approval. Credits: As soon as your cashback balance reaches $5, or 500 points, start redeeming your cash back.

The Ride Pass price cap

For many days during the month, Uber codes you find here will get you discounts. But how about a guaranteed price for all qualifying rides for a month? This price could include a discount if applicable. That's what you get for both uberPOOL and uberX with the Ride Pass. There's no similar guarantee that you'll get the pass. Only a select number of riders are eligible for the Ride Pass each month. To lock in a low price, apply. It's well worth the effort. Apply in the Ride Pass section of the app Wait for an email confirmation Confirmation is per month / 28 days The pass is available for select cities Offers and maximum values vary by location If confirmed, pay an upfront fee Get covered for trips costing less than the guaranteed price Some riders get offered auto-renewing ride passes. These lock in prices for a whole 12 weeks. Look in the app Ride Pass section to find out if your pass is auto-renewing.

Be a VIP automatically

Take more than 10 rides in a month and get VIP perks for the following month. Receive the perks automatically if the card on your account is the American Express Platinum Card. What do you get? Choice of top-rated drivers and vehicles Advance entrance to special offers The monthly newsletter including VIP discounts You don't even have to apply. Your ride frequency and location will automatically activate VIP. It also doesn't matter how many Uber coupons you used - the rides still count. How to verify your status: Select a destination in the app View the available car menu Slide the menu to the left If you see uberX VIP, you have the program enabled The program is available in New York City, New Orleans, Atlanta, Dallas and New Jersey, and is growing.
